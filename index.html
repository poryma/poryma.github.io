About

My research examines how social environments shape health inequalities, with particular attention to mortality, mental health, and suicide in South Korea and beyond. I use computational text analysis, quantitative longitudinal methods, and time-use data to trace how social disorganization, community violence, and environmental hazards accumulate into durable health disparities. I am especially interested in how social context affects aging populations and the unequal distribution of mortality risk.
